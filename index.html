About Me

Hi, I'm Brian. I currently live in Boulder, Colorado. Although I am originally from Colorado I grew up in the south and later spent most of my adult life in Chicago. I've been working in the fitness industry as a personal trainer for 12 years. As a trainer I've worked in almost every setting imaginable, small gyms, big gyms, athletic training centers, garages, parks and more. Three years ago I decided to work for myself so I've been operating full time as an independent trainer.

My hobbies include fitness (of course), coding, rock climbing, and generally anything that allows me to hang out with my dog Addison the rotterman.
